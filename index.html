How to Play Tabletop Craps

This is a simplified version of Craps, perfect for a quick game!

Rules

  1. Roll two dice.
  2. **First Roll:**
    • If you roll a 7, you win!
    • Any other number becomes the 'losing number'.
  3. **Subsequent Rolls:**
    • If you roll the 'losing number', you lose.
    • If you roll a 7, you lose.
    • Any other number, pass the dice to the next player.
